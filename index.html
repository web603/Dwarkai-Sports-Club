Welcome to Dwarkai Sports Club, Jalgaon’s premier destination for Box Cricket. Located conveniently behind Mahadev Nagar Kanalda Road (Near Bypass Highway NH 53, Gath No 468), our turf is designed to elevate your cricketing experience to professional levels.

We feature top-tier synthetic turf that provides excellent cushioning for joint protection, combined with high-lumen LED floodlights that perfectly mimic daylight for night matches. Our facility includes clean restrooms, hygienic changing rooms, and ample secure parking for all visitors.

Whether you're hosting a competitive local tournament, practicing with your academy, or just blowing off steam in a weekend match with friends, Dwarkai Sports Club guarantees a flawless, high-energy environment.
