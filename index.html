Matches & Sessions

Built by Athletes,
For Athletes.

Welcome to Dwarkai Sports Club, Jalgaon’s premier destination for Cricket. Located conveniently behind Mahadev Nagar Kanalda Road (Near Bypass Highway NH 53, Gath No 468), our facility is designed to elevate your game to professional standards.

We feature premium synthetic and matting pitches that provide excellent bounce and safety, paired with high-lumen LED floodlights for night cricket matches. Our club is fully affiliated with Nagpur, Mumbai, and Pune clubs connected directly to **MCA / VCA**, opening development pathways for aspiring regional athletes.

Whether you are looking for elite coaching, regular net booking, or booking our spacious day-night box layout with your group, Dwarkai Sports Club offers an unmatched athletic environment.
